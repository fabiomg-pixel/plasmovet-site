New publication · Frontiers in Immunology 2025

Diet-induced obese and diabetic host phenotype reduces mosquito ZIKV infections.

Blood from diet-induced obese and diabetic mice remodels Aedes aegypti gut metabolism and reduces Zika virus infections, establishing a novel immunometabolic axis in arbovirus transmission.

Menezes A, Walter-Nuno AB, Costa-Bartuli E et al. →
Landmark · PNAS 2021

Innate immune memory in primed mosquitoes.

Double peroxidase and histone acetyltransferase AgTip60 maintain immune memory in Anopheles — the first mechanistic description of trained immunity in vectors.

Gomes FM et al. →
Review · J Mol Evol 2024

Evolution of innate immunity across animals.

Comprehensive analyses tracing the major components of innate immunity across animal evolution, connecting mammalian trained immunity to invertebrate systems.

Silva RCM & Gomes FM →
